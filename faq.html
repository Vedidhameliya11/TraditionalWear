Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is TraditionWear?
A: TraditionWear is an online platform offering traditional Indian clothing for all occasions, supporting local artisans and promoting cultural fashion.
Q2: How can I place an order?
A: Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout using our secure payment system.
Q3: Do you offer cash on delivery?
A: Yes, we offer cash on delivery for selected locations in India.
Q4: What is the return policy?
A: You can return any item within 7 days of delivery if it is unused and in its original packaging. Please contact our support team for help.
Q5: How do I contact customer support?
A: Visit our Contact Us page or email us at support@traditionwear.com for assistance.
